## Introduction

The Tags component is designed to display a series of labels or tags, often used for categorization, filtering, or highlighting specific attributes. Each tag is presented as a distinct, styled element, making it easy for users to identify different categories or topics. The component is customizable, allowing for varied styles, colors, and sizes to suit the context. ## InnovativeTags

The InnovativeTags component uses the Badge component to display various tag styles, showcasing different visual variants like "default," "secondary," "destructive," and "outline." Each tag is styled with a slight margin to maintain spacing between tags, enhancing readability and organization. This component is perfect for labeling or categorizing items with distinct visual cues, making it easy for users to recognize the importance or type of each tag. The InnovativeTags component is well-suited for contexts where quick identification of different statuses or categories is needed, such as notifications, alerts, or item labels.
